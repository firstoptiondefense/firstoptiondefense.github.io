U.S. Military Veterans and certified Armed Security Professionals (40-hr trained) receive discounted pricing and a waived Day 1. You've already done the firearms safety work — we honor that and get straight to what matters for your Illinois CCW.

First Option Defense has always been known for welcoming women into firearms training. Our ladies-only classes offer a comfortable, judgment-free environment where you can learn at your own pace without feeling intimidated.
